CES LetterBox Chat

Hi! On this page you can chat with others conntected to the CES LetterBox. All chats are anonymous and not saved. Also, all chats are unmoderated, though we encourage everyone to be polite!

Please be aware that there may be individuals who are talking about things that may make you uncomfortable, but just as they are free to speak so too all of us are free to choose whether or not to participate. If someone says something that you choose to feel offended by the best course of action is to always take the higher road and disengage.
