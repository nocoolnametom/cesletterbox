CES LetterBox

Welcome!

Now, first of all, there is nothing illegal or scary going on here. This is a social place where you can chat and download files with people around you, anonymously!

You may also read the Letter to a CES Director, written by Jeremy Runnells. In the spring of 2013, Jeremy was approached and asked by a CES Director to share his concerns and questions about the LDS Church's origins, history, and current practices. In response, Jeremy wrote what later became publicly known as Letter to a CES Director.

This is an off-line network, specially designed and developed to provide access to the CES Letter in areas where individuals may browse it in privacy.

Even if you are not interested in reading the CES letter, you are welcome to stick around as long as you like.

The CES LetterBox is private; no logins are required and no user data is logged. To leave simply disconnect from the CES Letterbox's Wifi Network.

Please have fun, chat with people, and feel free to browse and download any of the available files you may like.
